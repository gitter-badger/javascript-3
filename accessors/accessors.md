## Accessors [**Back**](./../README.md)

- Accessors functions for properties are not required.

#### 1. use `getVal()` and `setVal(val)`

```js
/**
 * bad
 */
dragon.age();
dragon.age(25);

/**
 * good
 */
dragon.getAge();
dragon.setAge(25);
```
